Expiry Rules Reference
Citi Miles / TYP — No expiry while cards remain active. 127,899 Citi Miles + 373,865 TYP = ~149,546 KF equivalent. At $0.022/mile that's ~$6,093 in idle value. Forfeited only on card cancellation.
DBS Reward Points — Expire 12 months from earn date (end of month). Transfer to KrisFlyer promptly — once in KF, the 3-year clock restarts.
HSBC Reward Points — No stated expiry while Revolution card is active. Transfer to KrisFlyer at 5,000 pts → 2,000 KF miles. Admin fee applies per transfer.
UOB UNI$ (PPV, Lady's Solitaire) — Expire 2 years from date of earn. Convert via UOB KrisFlyer transfer portal. Rate: 5 UNI$ → 2 KF miles. Check UOB app for oldest tranche's expiry.
UOB PRVI Miles (WLE) — No expiry while card is active. Direct 1:1 transfer to KrisFlyer.
KrisFlyer Miles — Expire 3 years from credit date. Check KrisFlyer statement at singaporeair.com. Set the expiry date above to get alerts.
Optimisation Tips
762,285 KF miles is serious firepower — don't let it sit. That's enough for 2× return SIN–LHR Business Class (131k each way × 4 = 524k) with 238k left over, or multiple regional J-class trips.
UOB UNI$ expire in 2 years from earn date. With 170,038 UNI$ (WA) and 46,275 UNI$ (WLE), check your UOB app for the oldest tranche's expiry date.
Citi TYP (Rewards + Prestige): 373,865 TYP = ~149,546 KF miles. Transfer in blocks of 25k to minimise fees. Both cards have no expiry while active.
Stack your earn rate. Use DBS Woman's World for online/Shopee (4 mpd), Citi Prestige for dining + overseas (up to 5 mpd), HSBC Revolution for contactless + dining (2 mpd), Citi PremierMiles for hotel/travel (2–3 mpd).
Transfer timing matters. Only move points to KrisFlyer when you have a redemption target — once in KF, the 3-year expiry clock starts.
Batch your transfers to cut fees. Citi charges per transfer (min 10,000 TYP blocks), HSBC charges S$25 flat. Always transfer large chunks.
Best SQ sweet spots: SIN–BKK Business (50k return), SIN–NRT Economy (35k one-way), SIN–LHR Business (131k one-way), SIN–SYD Business (95k one-way).
